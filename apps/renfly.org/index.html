RenFly: Ren'Py visual novels in Flutter

RenFly is a Ren'Py visual novel player for on web, desktop, and mobile.

▶ OPEN PLAYER ✎ OPEN EDITOR

About

RenFly is the player app of FiestaVN, an open-source project for playing Ren'Py visual novels on any platform. It loads Ren'Py games and runs them everywhere Flutter runs: in the browser, on the desktop, and on mobile.

RenSpine: RenFly and Spine for animated visual novels

RenSpine pairs RenFly with Esoteric Software's Spine so characters are skeletal-animated with skins and animation mixing instead of static sprites.

▶ PLAY ✎ EDIT

Streaming

Games migrated to the .fly format can be streamed: the player downloads the script first and fetches images and audio only as the story needs them, so play starts without waiting for the whole game. Classic .rpy games need a one-time migration before they can stream.
